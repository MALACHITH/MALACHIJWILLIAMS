Recap of Case Study

Investment in cleaner functions of society.

Things like energy, meat, transportation, and housing are very inefficient when it comes to preserving the environment. If we want to combat climate change, we need to make these systems more sustainable and less destructive to the environment.

Most Effective

Renovating transportation and housing.

We have everything we need to renovate the infrastructure of our houses and our transportation. Making these things more eco-friendly is a necessary and relatively easy step towards reducing our impact on the environment. Aggressive mandates and investment are all we need.

Use of Technology

The meat industry and energy sector

Renovating the meat industry with the technology of lab-grown meat is another solution to the efficiency problem posed by factory farming. In addition, we need to further invest in clean energy in order to combat our reliance on foil fuels.

Project Implications

If I want my game to educate the player on these topics, I will need to include this information somehow in my game. How I will incorporate this into my game is yet to be seen.
