Problem Statement

Many of our necessary institutions like meat production, cars, and housing, contribute a lot to climate change, which presents an issue of an unavoidable production of carbon dioxide. Farms produce 14.5% of carbon emissions worldwide. A typical passenger car releases 4.6 metric tons of CO2 each year, with 1.4 billion cars in use.

Need Statement

We need to invest in cleaner ways to sustain the necessary functions of society. Meat can be produced in labs with much more efficiency and speed, cars can be replaced with public transit, and houses can be made more efficient by packing them tightly in city blocks.
